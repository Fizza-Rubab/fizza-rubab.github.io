I am a research intern at Carnegie Mellon University in Xu Lab, where I work on computer vision, biomedical image analysis and machine learning. I am also a Machine Learning Engineer at Farmevo.

At Xu Lab, I am working on Image Denoising, Cryo-ET classification and segmentation and Particle Picking. At Farmevo, I have worked on Crop Detection and Segmentation, Semi Supervised learning and Image Alignment and Registration. I did my Bachelors at Habib University where I received the Chancellor's Yohsin Award, Dean's Medal and the Best Computer Science Research Award.
